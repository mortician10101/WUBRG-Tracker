WUBRG Tracker

A fast, private Magic: The Gathering collection tracker for Android.

What this app is for

WUBRG Tracker helps Magic: The Gathering players catalogue and manage their card collections. You add cards by scanning them with your phone camera or by searching the card catalog, organize them into binders and decks, track each card's condition, quantity and purchase price, and see live market values and gains/losses over time. Everything is stored on your device and can be backed up to a spreadsheet in your own Google account.

Key features

  • Scan cards with on-device text recognition, or add them from the full catalog.
  • Organize cards into binders, decks, a wishlist, and a trade binder.
  • Live market prices (via Scryfall), converted to your currency, with value history.
  • Export your trade binder to Excel, CSV, or PDF.
  • Optional sync to a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive — usable across devices.
  • Built-in multiplayer life counter for your games.

Your data & privacy

WUBRG Tracker has no servers of its own. Your collection lives on your device and, if you enable sync, in a spreadsheet inside your own Google Drive — the app can only access the file it creates for you. Card scanning is processed on your device. There are no ads and no third-party tracking.
